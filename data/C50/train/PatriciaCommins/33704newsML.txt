The Battle Creek, Mich. based company blamed competitive promotions by others in the cereal category for the decline in its third quarter profits. We will continue to direct our efforts away from deep price promotion however, we are unwilling to see our leadership position comprised, Chairman Arnold Langbo said in a statement issued late Wednesday.
