Diamond Shamrock Inc said it plans to close its merger with Ultramar Corp on December and announce a new organizational structure shortly thereafter. In a meeting with security analysts, chief executive officer Roger Hemminghaus said Diamond Shamrock is close to the sale of its telecommunications business and its natural gas exploration and production operations in Bolivia. Hemminghaus also said that the new Ultramar Diamond Shamrock Corp will eagerly scour the industry for refining and marketing acquisitions and joint ventures. There are bound to be some opportunities that are out there for us, Hemminghaus said. Asked whether his new company would be interested in a joint venture similar to others taking place in the industry, Hemminghaus said You bet we would . He flatly dismissed speculation that Diamond Shamrock entered the pact with Ultramar because it was being targeted as an acquisition candidate. This was in no way a defensive move on our part, he said, adding Diamond Shamrock was not approached by another firm. He also said Diamond Shamrock had considered a transaction with Unocal Corp, which on Monday announced the sale of downstream assets to Tosco Corp.
